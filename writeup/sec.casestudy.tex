\subsection{Latent Dirichlet Allocation}

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) is one of the most popular topic models (\cite{blei2003latent}). It is used to find a hidden structure in documents, called \emph{topics}. Such topics are, mathematically speaking, probability distributions over a vocabulary. They are often presented as a list of words having the highest probabilities. The following paragraphs will explain the probabilistic model of LDA and how it is converted to an ERM.
